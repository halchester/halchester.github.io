Mountain conditions


Mountain conditions influence the weather. When prevailing winds carrying water vapor are blocked by mountains, the areas behind those mountains may not have enough or little rain compared to the windward side as mountains are laid as a protective shield against the air. For instance in Myanmar, as Arakan mountain range and Tanintharyi mountain range act as protective shield, the windward sides of Arakan State and Tanintharyi devision may have excessive amount of rain.
